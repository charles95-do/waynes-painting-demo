Our Story

Who We Are

Waynes Painting and Home Improvement LLC is a locally owned and operated contracting company based in Mechanicsville, Maryland, in the heart of St. Mary's County. We specialize in residential painting and home improvement services, delivering honest work at fair prices to homeowners across Southern Maryland.

What started with a paintbrush and a promise to do things right has grown into a full-service home improvement operation. From interior and exterior painting to kitchen and bathroom remodels, deck construction, and pressure washing, we bring the same attention to detail to every job -- big or small.

We're proud to be BBB A+ accredited (2025) and fully licensed and insured. Our reputation is built on word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied customers who know they can count on us for quality craftsmanship, clear communication, and a clean job site.

When you hire Waynes Painting, you're hiring your neighbor. We live here, we work here, and we stand behind every project we complete.
